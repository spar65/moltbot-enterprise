## Subscription Overview

VibeCoder offers five subscription tiers to meet different needs and budgets:

| Tier    | Monthly Price | Best For                                     |
| ------- | ------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Free    | $0            | Getting started and exploring basic features |
| Basic   | $5            | Individual users with simple projects        |
| Sync    | $15           | Teams and collaborative projects             |
| Cleanup | $45           | Professional users with complex projects     |
| Elite   | $100          | Organizations with enterprise needs          |

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time.

## Detailed Tier Descriptions

### Free Tier

The Free tier is perfect for individuals who want to explore VibeCoder's basic capabilities without any financial commitment.

**Includes:**

- Access to basic templates and tools
- Create and manage up to 3 projects
- Community support through forums
- Basic vibe coding features

**Limitations:**

- No access to premium templates
- No analytics or export options
- No team collaboration features

### Basic Tier ($5/month)

The Basic tier is designed for individual users who need more storage and access to premium templates.

**Includes everything in Free, plus:**

- Unlimited saved projects
- Access to premium templates
- Basic analytics dashboard
- Export projects in multiple formats
- Email support

**Ideal for:**

- Freelancers
- Individual developers
- Students and hobbyists

### Sync Tier ($15/month)

The Sync tier adds powerful collaboration features, making it perfect for small teams working together.

**Includes everything in Basic, plus:**

- Team collaboration with up to 5 members
- Shared projects and workspaces
- Version history and change tracking
- Team analytics and insights
- Collaborative editing features
- Priority email support

**Ideal for:**

- Small development teams
- Startups
- Creative agencies
- Educational groups

### Cleanup Tier ($45/month)

The Cleanup tier offers advanced tools for professional users who need to handle complex projects and large datasets.

**Includes everything in Sync, plus:**

- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Bulk operations and batch processing
- Advanced cleanup tools for code optimization
- API access for custom integrations
- Team collaboration with up to 15 members
- Phone support during business hours

**Ideal for:**

- Professional developers
- Medium-sized teams
- Agencies with multiple clients
- Technical consultancies

### Elite Tier ($100/month)

The Elite tier provides enterprise-grade features for organizations with advanced needs and larger teams.

**Includes everything in Cleanup, plus:**

- Custom rules engine for organization-specific requirements
- White labeling options for client-facing projects
- Priority support with dedicated account manager
- Enterprise SSO integration
- Team collaboration with unlimited members
- Custom onboarding and training
- 24/7 priority support

**Ideal for:**

- Large organizations
- Enterprise development teams
- Companies with complex compliance requirements
- Educational institutions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I upgrade my subscription?

1. Log in to your VibeCoder account
2. Go to Account Settings > Subscription
3. Click "Upgrade Plan"
4. Select your desired tier
5. Enter payment information
6. Confirm your upgrade

### Can I change tiers later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, your new tier will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### How does the 14-day trial work?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel anytime before then with no charge. You'll need to provide payment information to start the trial, but we won't bill you until the trial ends.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as PayPal.

### Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with your subscription within the first 30 days, contact our support team for a full refund.

### How do team seats work?

The Sync tier includes 5 team members, the Cleanup tier includes 15 team members, and the Elite tier includes unlimited team members. Additional seats can be purchased for the Sync and Cleanup tiers at $3 per user per month.

### What happens to my projects if I downgrade?

If you downgrade to a tier with fewer features, you'll retain access to all your projects, but premium features will be disabled. For example, if you downgrade from Sync to Basic, your shared projects will become private, but the content will be preserved.
